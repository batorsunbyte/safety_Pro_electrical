# Google Business Profile — Setup & Review Guide (for Arif)

> **Why this matters more than anything else.** The website makes people trust you once they
> land on it. But the single biggest source of *new* organic electrical jobs is the **Google
> Map Pack** — the 3 map results at the top of Google. Ranking there is driven mostly by your
> **Google reviews**. You already have 40+ happy customers on hipages — let's turn that into
> Google reviews. This is ~30 minutes of setup for a return that compounds for years.

Target: **10–15 Google reviews in the first 2 weeks.** That alone often jumps you above local
competitors.

---

## Part 1 — Create your Google Business Profile (~15 min)

1. Go to **google.com/business** and sign in with the Google account you want to manage it with
   (ideally `safetypro.electrical@gmail.com`).
2. **Business name:** `Safety Pro Electrical` (exactly — don't add keywords like "electrician
   Cranbourne", Google can suspend for that).
3. **Business category:** choose **Electrician** as the primary category. Later you can add
   secondary categories like *Electrical installation service*.
4. **Do you have a location customers can visit?** Choose **No** — you go to customers. This
   makes you a **Service-Area Business** and hides your home address.
5. **Service areas:** add your real suburbs:
   `Cranbourne East, Cranbourne, Clyde North, Clyde, Narre Warren, Berwick, Officer, Pakenham,
   Dandenong, Endeavour Hills, Hallam, Noble Park, Frankston` (add more as you like).
6. **Contact details:**
   - Phone: `0490 101 365`
   - Website: your live site URL (e.g. `https://safetyproelectrical.com.au`)
7. **Verification:** Google will ask to verify (usually by phone/SMS or video). Do it straight
   away — you can't appear on Maps until you're verified.

### Fill it out completely (this ranks you higher)

- **Hours:** set your real opening hours. Turn on "Open 24 hours" only if you truly take calls
  24/7, otherwise set normal hours and mention emergencies in the description.
- **Description** (paste and tweak):
  > Safety Pro Electrical is a licensed A-Grade electrical business serving South-East Melbourne.
  > From emergency call-outs and switchboard upgrades to EV charger installations, safety
  > switches, smoke alarms, lighting and full rewires — we deliver prompt, honest, fully
  > licensed work backed by a Certificate of Electrical Safety. A-Grade licensed (A68377),
  > Registered Electrical Contractor (REC-33712), fully insured.
- **Services:** add each service (Emergency Electrician, Switchboard Upgrades, EV Charger
  Installation, Safety Switches & Smoke Alarms, Lighting & Power Points, Rewiring). These match
  the website.
- **Photos:** upload **10–15 real photos** — finished switchboards, EV chargers, downlights,
  your ute, you/the team on the job. Real photos massively boost trust and ranking. (Send these
  to Zakir too — they'll go on the website gallery.)

## Part 2 — Get reviews from past customers (the money step)

Once verified, Google gives you a **short review link**:
- In your Business Profile → **Ask for reviews** → copy the link (looks like `g.page/r/...`).

Send it to past happy customers. Keep it personal and short. Here are ready-to-send WhatsApp
messages — just paste in the review link:

### Template A — warm & short
> Hi [Name], it's Arif from Safety Pro Electrical ⚡ Thanks again for having me out for your
> [job, e.g. switchboard upgrade]. If you were happy with the work, would you mind leaving a
> quick Google review? It genuinely helps a local business like mine. Takes 30 seconds: [LINK]
> Really appreciate it 🙏

### Template B — even shorter
> Hi [Name], Arif here from Safety Pro Electrical. Would you mind leaving a quick Google review
> of the job I did for you? It helps us a lot 🙏 Here's the link: [LINK] — thank you!

### Template C — for very happy customers
> Hi [Name], so glad you're happy with the [job]! If you've got 30 seconds, a Google review
> would mean the world and helps other locals find a sparky they can trust: [LINK] Cheers, Arif ⚡

**Tips:**
- Send to **5 people a day** rather than all at once (a sudden burst of reviews can look
  unnatural to Google).
- Message the customers who gave the best hipages feedback first — they already love your work.
- Always reply to every review you get (a quick "Thanks [Name], appreciate it!" is enough).
  Responding is itself a ranking signal.

---

## Part 3 — Keep it alive (5 min/week)

- Post a **photo of a recent job** every week or two via the "Add update" button.
- Add new services/photos as you go.
- Reply to every review, good or bad, politely.
